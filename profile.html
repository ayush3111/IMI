Step 1:

Choose a Design

The first step in the tattooing process is the selection of a design. Most tattoo shops will have pre-drawn pictures, also called "flash".

Step 2:

Placement

This step can come into play before or after choosing your design, but the placement of tattoo is important. Choose wisely.

Step 3:

Get the Tattoo

Once you've chosen your design and talked with the artist, he or she will typically make a ditto or an outline for your skin.

Step 4:

Prepare for Pain

There's no getting around it: the tattooing process involves needles which prick the skin hundreds of times as the ink is placed.

Step 5:

Aftercare

It's normal for the area around the tattoo to look a little red when the tattooing process is done.Bleeding should stop fairly quickly.
